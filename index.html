Projects

Oto-Run

Oto is stranded on a planet and has been instruced to do one thing: Run the planet. Blocking his path are obstacles that he must avoid to continue his mission. Built using Java and jMonkey.

ParKey

Paying for parking sucks. What sucks more is paying for time that you may not use. Built at Pennapps, ParKey is a iOS and Arduino application that allows user to pay for their parking per minute by syncing to a bluetooth meter.

Outage Outreach Service

Nothing is worse than finding out when you get home that your internet service is out. To help solve the issue of angry customers and provide courtesy, We developed a SMS notification system integrating existing platform services to notify customers of a service outage.

SecureMi

Journalists in the Middle East often encounter security check points. If any information on their phones is deemed a threat, Journalists could be interrogated, tortured or killed. Built at Google's Hack 4 Humanity, SecureMi is a custom lock screen that upon entering a security pin, safely hides all of the user's media files.
